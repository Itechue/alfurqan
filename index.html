DIRECTOR | SHEIKH YAHAYA ABUBAKAR KANGIWA

DIRECTOR'S Message

On behalf of the staff of Sarki Mudi Academy, I am happy to welcome you to the 2023/2024 academic year. We are looking forward to a productive partnership with you to ensure that our children achieve their highest potential.

We know that a strong partnership with parents makes a significant difference in a child’s education. As partners, we share the responsibility for our children’s success and will do our part. Our theme for this academic session is PROMOTING EXCELLENCE THROUGH DETERMINATION AND HARD WORK.

Please ensure that your child:

  • Attends school daily and arrives on time
  • Completes homework and projects diligently.
  • Reads daily to improve literacy skills.
  • Shares school experiences with you.
  • Informs you of any additional support needed.

Why Choose Us?

The school tenaciously holds the view that,early education is the best.In veiw of this, conscious effort is made toward equipping the child with sound islamic values as the foundation on which technological and scientific education rests.

Our Vission and Mission

Mission Statement

To enable all learners to access learning through the provision of differentiated in depth and cohesive learning programs aligned to year level content achievement standard informed by the Nigeria National curriculum and to produce excellent student in both islamic and western education.

Vision statement

It is the vision of Sarki Mudi Academy to pupils/student that will acquire complete and comprehensive knowledge in islam and Western Education,comparable to other International Schools in Nigeria and the world at large.
