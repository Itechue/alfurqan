About Us

Learn More About Our Work And Our Cultural Activities

Sarki Mudi Academy adpots the modern western/islamic teaching methods into a fun, entertaining culture of learning. Our service spans the k 12 gategories with special education programs for special needs children services.

Nursery

Nursery: In our nursery program, we cater to children aged 1-2 years. Here, the focus is on building essential skills, fostering independence, and introducing early learning concepts in a play-based setting.

What sets our service apart:

- Qualified and Caring Staff: Our team of experienced and certified educators is passionate about early childhood education and dedicated to your child's well-being.

- Safe and Stimulating Environment: We maintain a secure and enriching setting where children can explore, learn, and grow.

- Engaging Curriculum: Our curriculum is designed to be age-appropriate, providing the right balance of play, learning, and creative expression.

- Parent Involvement: We value the partnership with parents and offer regular updates on your child's progress, so you are always connected to their development.

- Enrichment Activities: We provide opportunities for children to explore various activities, including arts and crafts, music, physical education, and more.

We understand that every child is unique and deserves the best start in life. Our Nursery and Primary service is committed to providing a nurturing, educational, and fun environment where your child can thrive and embark on a journey of lifelong learning. We look forward to being a part of your child's early educational experience.

Primary School

Welcome to our Primary School, where the journey of education takes its next significant step! Our primary school service is designed to provide a strong educational foundation while nurturing your child's curiosity, creativity, and character.

At our primary school, we understand the critical role this stage plays in a child's development. Here's a glimpse of what you can expect:

1. Comprehensive Curriculum: Our primary school curriculum is thoughtfully designed to provide a well-rounded education. We focus on core subjects such as mathematics, language arts, science, and social studies, while also offering enriching classes in art, music, physical education, and more.

2. Experienced Educators: Our dedicated and certified teaching staff is committed to fostering a love of learning in your child. We provide a supportive and engaging learning environment where students are encouraged to ask questions and explore their interests.

3. Personalized Learning: We understand that every child is unique. Our approach is personalized to cater to each student's individual needs and learning styles, ensuring they reach their full potential.

4. Character Development: Beyond academics, we emphasize the importance of character education. Our students learn essential values like respect, responsibility, empathy, and integrity to become well-rounded individuals.

5. Extracurricular Activities: We offer a wide range of extracurricular activities, from sports and clubs to cultural and artistic pursuits, allowing students to explore their passions and talents.

6. Parent Involvement: We value the partnership between parents and the school. We keep you informed about your child's progress and encourage your active participation in their educational journey.

Our primary school is more than a place of learning; it's a community where children develop skills, friendships, and a lifelong love for knowledge. We are committed to providing a safe, nurturing, and stimulating environment that prepares your child for the challenges and opportunities of the future.

With a focus on academic excellence, character development, and a nurturing atmosphere, our primary school is the ideal place for your child's educational growth. We look forward to being a part of your child's academic journey and helping them lay the foundation for a successful and fulfilling future.
